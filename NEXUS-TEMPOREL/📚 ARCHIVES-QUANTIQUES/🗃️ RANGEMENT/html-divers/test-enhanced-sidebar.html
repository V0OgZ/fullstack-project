🧪 How to Test

  1. Open the Heroes of Time application at http://localhost:3000
  2. Look for the enhanced sidebar on the right side
  3. Check the resource display in the sidebar header
  4. Click the ⚔️ button to test the Enhanced Hero Panel
  5. Click the 🏰 button to test the Enhanced Castle Panel
  6. Click the 🎒 button to test the Enhanced Inventory Panel
  7. Click the 🐉 button to test the Epic Content Viewer
  8. Verify that real images load (or fallback emojis appear)
  9. Test switching between different tabs and panels
  10. Check that action buttons respond to clicks
